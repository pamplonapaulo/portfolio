The story of Paulo Pamplona

It's a story of languages.

I'm a former public relations now in a new career as a web developer. But I like to think that I'm a lover of languages. After 10 years dealing with media language, now I plan to spend at least 10 other years dealing with machine languages.

Programming languages are the final fronteir of my literacy. Why? Well, despite good skills in my mother tongue, to learn English made me feel much less illiterate, considering the huge amount of English content at the World Wide Web. So now learning programming languages makes me feel like learning to read and write again. We live in a digital society and soon we might have more people writting apps than books. I'm seriouslly committed to become one of them.
